Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

1. Not opening any suspicious links as they could be malicious and designed to steal our information (like our usernames, passwords, credit card numbers). Or we might download a harmful program that can damage our computer or steal our files/information.
2. Keep the website's software and codes updated with the latest fixes to prevent malicious people from taking advantage of known problems.
3. Use strong passwords and special ways to make sure only the right people can access the website.
4. Implement HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure). It will encrypt all data that is transmitted between the website and the user's browser. This will prevent attackers from intercepting sensitive information.
5. Regularly backup website's data as it will ensure that in the event of an attack data can be restored and the website can be up and running quickly.
6. Educate users about safe browsing habits and how to identify and avoid phishing attacks.
